Interesting facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among Chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting facts about Pigeons

  2. Pigeons arre renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities

Interesting facts about Cicada's

  1. Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  2. Cicadas are known for their buzzing and clicking sounds
  3. Cicadas have prominent compound eyes set wide apart on the sides of the head
